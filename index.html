About

Full Stack Web Developer

I am a versitile full stack developer with a broad range of software development experience. I really enjoy learning new things. I have graduated from the UW coding bootcamp where I have been honing my web development skills in JavaScript, and the MERN stack. The boot camp was an intensive 24 week program dedicated to the design and construction of web applications using some of the latest technology.

I worked as a software developer on the customer relationship management system that support the cessation program. The program was primarily driven through phone based coaching and online learning. I am deeply grateful for having been part of a business that impacted so many lives.

I am passionate about staying in shape through hiking and cycling. I organize hikes for my friends and family. I enjoy volunteering with a grade school tutoring program.
